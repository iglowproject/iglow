iGlow Conference

The iGlow Conference is an annual event organized by LoveWorks LUN, to connect social innovators, researchers, leaders and players of SDGs 1, 2, 3, 4, 9, 11 and 17, to present their novel works/ideas/initiatives solving SDG-needs in a community.

It aims at educating and empowering social innovators and aspiring ones with knowledge and tools for more sustainable social innovations in their communities. The iGlow Conference also creates a platform for visibility and networking; exposure to current pressing social challenges and proposed solutions; opportunities for collaboration; research funding and much more.

We’d like to announce that IGLOW 2023 is now a 1-day event (not 2 days). It’ll be held on Friday, April 28, 2023. Registration closes on April 25th. All registered participants should kindly check their emails for login details.

Please, ensure to register with an active email, and frequently check your emails for updates

IICSI 2023: Topics/Highlights

  • The Future of Education;
  • Redesigning Education >> VR, AR, Metaverse
  • UnpackGPT - exploring ChatGPT.
  • Leveraging AI to accelerate Social Innovation.
  • Fundings and Opportunities for social projects and social innovations.

IICSI 2023: What to Expect

The sessions of IICSI 2023 are tailored to help both old and new players in the social innovation space to achieve the following;

  • Cross Domain Innovation; Principles and ideas for applying Cross domain thinking in your social enterprise/initiatve.
  • Technologies for Social Innovators; Tools for tech and non-tech social innovators to address social problems facing humanity.
  • Opportunities for new Players; Opportunities in sustainable development goals (SDGs) or social entrepreneurship.
  • Research ideas and Opportunities; Research frontiers, as well as current problems facing SDGs, ideas and funding opportunities.
  • Networking Opportunities; Opportunities to identify projects of interest with networking or partnership possibilities.
  • Certificate of Attendance; Attendance Certificates available to registered participant (full attendance mandatory).
