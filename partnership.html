Greetings,

We are pleased to invite social enterprises or institutions passionate about social innovations, for partnership with the iGLOW 2023 Conference. The conference aims to connect, educate, equip, and empower social innovators, researchers, leaders, and players of SDGs 1, 2, 3, 4, 9, 11 and 17, to present their novel works/ideas/initiatives solving SDG-needs in a community.

The iGlow Conference also creates a platform for visibility and networking; exposure to current pressing social challenges and proposed solutions; opportunities for collaboration; research funding and much more.

We are inviting organizations to partner with us in a variety of ways, including:

  • Research Funding: Identify talents or promising ideas at IICSI 2023, and support their implementation
  • Brand Visibility: Supporting and amplifying iGlow Project within your network for publicity and sponsorship
  • Non-Financial or In-Kind Donations: These include incentives such as events space, products and services that can help achieve our mission and create a platform for change-makers to connect and collaborate.

Best Regards

The iGLOW Conference Organizing Committee
